Operations management

To perform the Operation level operations, a service must be selected. To select a service choose an item on the drop down list menu located over the toolbar. The Operation level operations are carried out through link reported in the second row of the toolbar. This links are displayed only when a service is selected.

The TOOLBOX can be used in other context than just the SSE environment. In the following we refer to "generic operations" as operations which are not compliant with the SSE ICD or with the EOLI ICD.

All the operations described in the Operation management sub-sections will be performed on the selected service.
