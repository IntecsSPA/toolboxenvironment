Conventions

The following conventions are used in this user manual:

  Indicates a note with useful information allowing you to perform your task faster and more efficient and to avoid mistakes.
  Indicates a caution, attracting attention to a frequent source of mistakes or problems. Not taking into account the caution will lead to incorrect results or a malfunction.
  Indicates a warning. Ignoring a warning may lead to important loss of data or incorrect behaviour of the system.
