Contacts & error report

To report a problem encountered during the usage of the Toolbox, please contact the Toolbox support team.

Please give as much details as possible, including history of commands, complete error messages and other values relevant to the error.  Screen dumps are appreciated.

Please address all your reports and all your requests to:

Email: toolbox@intecs.it

You can always download the latest version of the Toolbox, retrieve the source core and access to the updated documentation by following this link:

http://code.google.com/p/toolboxenvironment/
